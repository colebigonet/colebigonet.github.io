Cole Bigonet

Hi I’m Cole Bigonet (pronounced Bee-zho-nay), an artist of many forms, mainly musical and visual. First off, I’m crazy for the ukulele. There is nothing I’m more passionate about, playing every chance I get. An avid composer as well, I have created many original pieces for the ukulele. I am inspired by Jake Shimabukuro, a ukulele virtuoso who asked me to play my composition for him in November 2015, after I saw him in concert in NYC. He said, "You definitely have the mind of a composer."

In addition to music, I am interested in the visual arts such as drawing, painting, digital art, and ceramics. Someday, I would like to tour as a professional ukulele player, make my own albums, and continue to explore visual arts as well. Recently, I started to learn how to program, and I created this site as part of that effort.
